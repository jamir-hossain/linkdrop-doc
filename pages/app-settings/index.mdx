### App Settings

<p className="mt-4">
  In the App Settings section, you'll find several essential options to configure your application's key features for optimal functionality. These include:
</p>

- **Global Settings:** Manage the overall configuration of your application. <br/>
- **SMTP Settings:** Enable email functionality, such as sending registration emails to your users. <br/>
- **Google Authentication:** Allow users to register or log in using their Google account for a seamless authentication experience. <br/>
- **Payment Setup:** Configure Stripe to activate and manage payment-related features efficiently.
These settings are crucial to ensure your application runs smoothly and provides a complete user experience.
